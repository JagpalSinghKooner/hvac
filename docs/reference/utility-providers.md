## Key Rebate Programs (Ontario-Wide)

### Provincial Programs

| Program | Provider | Eligible Equipment | Notes |
|---------|----------|-------------------|-------|
| **Enbridge Home Efficiency Rebate Plus** | Enbridge Gas | Furnaces, boilers, water heaters | Most common program for gas heating upgrades |
| **Save on Energy** | Province-wide (multiple LDCs) | Heat pumps, AC, smart thermostats | Electricity-focused rebate program |
| **Canada Greener Homes Grant** | Federal (via NRCan) | Heat pumps, insulation, windows | Up to $5,000 for energy upgrades |

### Key Terminology
- **LDC:** Local Distribution Company (electricity)
- **Enbridge Gas:** Dominant natural gas provider in Southern Ontario
- **Save on Energy:** Province-wide electricity conservation program administered by local LDCs

## Rebate Content Guidelines by City

### High-Value Cities (Larger populations, more programs)

**Burlington, Oakville, Milton:**
- Mention: Enbridge Home Efficiency Rebate Plus (up to $2,500)
- Mention: Save on Energy (heat pump rebates up to $6,200)
- Target audience: Affluent homeowners upgrading from older systems

**Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo:**
- Mention: Enbridge Home Efficiency Rebate Plus
- Mention: Save on Energy (university town energy consciousness)
- Angle: Tech-savvy homeowners interested in smart home integration

**Guelph:**
- Mention: Enbridge Home Efficiency Rebate Plus
- Mention: Save on Energy via Guelph Hydro
- Angle: Municipal utility partnership, community-focused messaging

### Mid-Size Towns (Strong community identity)

**Georgetown, Fergus, Elora, Orangeville:**
- Mention: Enbridge rebates (primary focus)
- Mention: Save on Energy (secondary)
- Angle: Small-town trust, long-term customer relationships

### Small Towns/Rural Communities

**Elmira, St. Jacobs, New Hamburg, Arthur, Mount Forest, Erin, Hillsburgh, Shelburne, Listowel, Palmerston, Drayton, Harriston:**
- Focus: Enbridge Home Efficiency Rebate Plus
- Angle: Practical savings for rural homeowners, heating cost reduction
- Note: Some rural areas may have propane as alternative to natural gas (verify on project basis)

## Usage Notes for Content Generation

1. **City-Specific Accuracy:** Always reference the correct LDC name for electricity when mentioning Save on Energy program
2. **Rebate Amounts:** Amounts listed are maximums; actual rebates vary by equipment AFUE/SEER ratings
3. **Program Availability:** All cities have access to Enbridge (gas) and Save on Energy (electricity) programs, but messaging should emphasize the most relevant programs for each city's demographic
4. **Premium Positioning:** Frame rebates as "smart investment" not "deals" (aligns with affluent target audience)
