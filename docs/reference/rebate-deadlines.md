## Active Programs

### Home Renovation Savings (HRS) Program â­ PRIMARY

**Status:** ACTIVE â€” Extended through November 2026
**Launched:** January 28, 2025
**Deadline:** November 30, 2026
**Partners:** Enbridge Gas + Save on Energy (IESO)
**Link:** [homerenovationsavings.ca](https://www.homerenovationsavings.ca/)

**Key Feature:** NO pre- or post-retrofit energy audit required. Faster, simpler process.

#### Heat Pump Rebates (by heating fuel type)

| Heating Type | Rebate | Maximum |
|--------------|--------|---------|
| Natural Gas (Enbridge) | $500/ton | $2,000 |
| Electric (Ontario grid) | $1,250/ton | $7,500 |
| Geothermal | $2,000/ton | $12,000 |

**Example:** 3-ton cold climate air source heat pump (ccASHP)
- Gas-heated home: $1,500 rebate
- Electric-heated home: $3,750 rebate

#### Multi-Measure Stream

| Upgrade Type | Maximum Rebate |
|--------------|----------------|
| Full home insulation bundle | Up to $7,700 |
| Attic insulation only | Up to $1,000 |

#### Eligibility
- Home located in Ontario
- Connected to Enbridge Gas OR provincial electricity grid
- Current heating: natural gas or electric resistance
- **NOT eligible:** Oil or propane heated homes (under HRS)

- Air sealing

- Solar panels
