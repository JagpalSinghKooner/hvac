Indoor units install in optimal locations delivering comfort without ductwork through finished spaces. Single outdoor units support 1-5 indoor air handlers, providing independent zone control where each space maintains desired temperatures regardless of other areas.

Multi-zone configurations allow different capacity units matching varied space requirements—12,000 BTU unit for master bedroom, 18,000 BTU for family room, 9,000 BTU for home office.
