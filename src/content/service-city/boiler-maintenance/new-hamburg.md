    Our boiler maintenance service begins with combustion analysis using calibrated instruments measuring oxygen content, carbon dioxide levels, carbon monoxide production, and flue gas temperatures. Control system verification confirms thermostat calibration, zone valve operation, outdoor reset functionality, and safety limit proper response. Electrical connections receive inspection and tightening—vibration loosens terminals over time, creating resistance that generates heat and eventual connection failure.
