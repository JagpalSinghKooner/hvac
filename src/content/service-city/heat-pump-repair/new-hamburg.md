    Our diagnostic approach begins with understanding symptom patterns and operating history before testing components. We verify staging configuration allows heat pump to carry heating load whenever capable, bringing furnace burners online only when outdoor temperature drops below equipment capacity or when indoor temperature cannot be maintained with heat pump alone. Two-fuel control systems are tested to confirm economic switching operates correctly, selecting the most cost-effective heating source given current electricity and gas rates. All repairs are tested under actual heating or cooling load to verify proper operation before leaving equipment in service.
