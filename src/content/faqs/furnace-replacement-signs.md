Common signs that your furnace may need replacing include:

- **Age:** Most furnaces last 15-20 years. If yours is approaching or past this age, replacement is often more cost-effective than ongoing repairs.
- **Frequent repairs:** If you're calling for repairs multiple times per season, the costs add up quickly.
- **Rising energy bills:** An inefficient furnace works harder and uses more fuel, increasing your heating costs.
- **Uneven heating:** Cold spots or temperature fluctuations throughout your home indicate declining performance.
- **Strange noises:** Banging, rattling, or squealing sounds often signal worn components.
