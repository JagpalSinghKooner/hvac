Yes, all our technicians are properly licensed, insured, and qualified to work on your heating and cooling systems:

**TSSA Certified:** Our technicians are certified by the Technical Standards and Safety Authority (TSSA), the regulatory body for fuel-burning equipment in Ontario.

**Fully Insured:** We carry comprehensive liability insurance with maximum coverage, protecting both our team and your property.

**WSIB Covered:** We maintain Workplace Safety and Insurance Board coverage, ensuring you're protected from liability for any workplace injuries.

**Industry Certifications:** We're recognized by the Better Business Bureau and hold HRAI (Heating, Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Institute of Canada) certification, including Heat Pump Champion status.

When you hire B.A.P Heating & Cooling, you're working with qualified professionals who take safety and quality seriously. We've been serving Ontario since 1994, building our reputation on dependable, professional service.
