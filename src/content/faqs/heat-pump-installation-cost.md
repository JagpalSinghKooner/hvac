Air-source heat pump installation in Ontario typically costs $8,000-$15,000, depending on:

- **Unit size:** Based on your home's heating/cooling needs
- **Efficiency rating:** Higher SEER/HSPF units cost more upfront but save more long-term
- **Installation complexity:** Existing ductwork, electrical requirements
- **Brand and features:** Variable-speed vs single-stage compressors

**Net cost after rebates:**
Current government programs offer substantial rebates for heat pump installations, often $5,000-$7,500 or more. This can reduce your net cost to $3,000-$10,000 depending on the system and your eligibility.
