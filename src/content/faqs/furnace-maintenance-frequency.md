We recommend having your furnace professionally serviced once per year, ideally before the heating season begins in fall. Regular annual maintenance helps prevent breakdowns, improves energy efficiency, and extends the lifespan of your equipment. During a maintenance visit, our technicians inspect all components, clean the system, and make necessary adjustments to ensure safe and efficient operation.
