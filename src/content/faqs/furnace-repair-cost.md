Furnace repair costs depend on the specific problem. Common repairs typically range from $150-$600:

**Common repair costs:**
- Igniter replacement: $150-$300
- Flame sensor cleaning/replacement: $100-$250
- Blower motor repair: $300-$600
- Thermostat issues: $100-$300
- Control board replacement: $400-$600+

**What affects the cost:**
- Part prices and availability
- Labour time required
- Furnace age and model
- Whether it's a standard or high-efficiency unit

**Our approach:**
We provide free diagnostics to identify the problem. Before any repair work begins, you'll receive a clear, upfront price with no hidden fees. If repairs aren't cost-effective given your furnace's age and condition, we'll tell you honestly and discuss replacement options.

**Emergency repairs:**
We offer 24/7 emergency service for furnace breakdowns. Same transparent pricing applies—you'll always know the cost before we proceed.
