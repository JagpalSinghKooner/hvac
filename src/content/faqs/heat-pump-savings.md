Most Ontario homeowners save 30-50% on heating costs after switching to a heat pump. Your actual savings depend on several factors:

**Biggest savings when replacing:**
- Electric baseboard heating (50%+ savings possible)
- Oil furnaces (40-50% savings typical)
- Propane furnaces (35-45% savings typical)
- Natural gas (20-35% savings typical)

**Factors affecting savings:**
- Current fuel costs and electricity rates
- Home insulation and air sealing
- Heat pump efficiency rating
- How cold your winter thermostat setting is
- Local climate variations

**Additional value:**
- Eliminates need for separate AC purchase
- Government rebates reduce upfront cost significantly
- Lower maintenance than combustion systems
- Increased home value

**Rebate programs:**
Current federal and provincial programs offer thousands of dollars toward heat pump installation. We handle all rebate paperwork and help you maximize available incentives.

During your free estimate, we can calculate projected savings based on your current heating bills and home characteristics.
